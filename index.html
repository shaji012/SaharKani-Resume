Sahar Kani

Biology

Sahar Kani is a dedicated and ambitious fourth-year Biology major at the University of California Riverside. With a passion for the intersection of science and healthcare, Sahar is actively pursuing a career in dentistry. Throughout her academic journey, Sahar has demonstrated a strong commitment to excellence and a drive for continuous learning. As a Biology major, she has honed her scientific knowledge and developed a solid foundation in the life sciences. Her coursework has equipped her with a deep understanding of biological concepts, research methodologies, and critical thinking skills. Beyond her academic pursuits, Sahar has actively engaged with the UC Riverside community. For the past two years, she has served as the Outreach Chair for the Pre-Dental Society, where she organized various events and activities to promote oral health education and awareness. In this role, Sahar displayed exceptional leadership and organizational skills, successfully coordinating workshops, seminars, and volunteer opportunities for society members. Sahar's commitment to fostering a sense of community extends beyond the Pre-Dental Society. She also served as a Program Advisor for the Middle Eastern Student Living Learning Community in Pentland Hills within Residential Life. In this capacity, she provided guidance and support to students, facilitated cultural programming, and helped create an inclusive and welcoming environment for all residents. Sahar's interpersonal skills, cultural sensitivity, and ability to connect with diverse groups of individuals made her an effective advocate for the students she served.
With a deep-rooted aspiration to attend dental school, Sahar is driven to make a positive impact in the field of dentistry. She possesses a genuine passion for oral health and its connection to overall well-being. Sahar is particularly interested in exploring how oral health intersects with systemic health and is motivated to contribute to advancements in dental care that benefit patients on a holistic level. In addition to her academic and extracurricular pursuits, Sahar actively seeks opportunities to expand her knowledge and skill set. She consistently seeks out volunteer experiences, shadowing opportunities, and continuing education courses to deepen her understanding of dentistry and gain practical exposure to patient care. Sahar believes in the importance of staying up-to-date with the latest advancements and research in the dental field to provide the highest quality of care to her future patients.

Sahar Kani's dedication, academic achievements, and involvement in the UC Riverside community make her a well-rounded and promising candidate for dental school. Her strong work ethic, leadership skills, and genuine passion for dentistry position her as an asset to any dental program. Sahar's unwavering commitment to improving oral health, coupled with her empathetic nature and dedication to patient care, sets her on a path to make a meaningful impact in the field of dentistry.
